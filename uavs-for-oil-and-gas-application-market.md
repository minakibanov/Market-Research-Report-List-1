<p><p>Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) in the oil and gas sector are categorized into three main types: fixed-wing, multi-rotor, and hybrid wing. Fixed-wing UAVs are suited for long-range surveying and monitoring due to their greater endurance and speed. Multi-rotor UAVs excel in vertical takeoff and landing, allowing for precise inspections in confined spaces. Hybrid wing UAVs combine features of both, providing versatility for both extended range and maneuverability, making them ideal for diverse applications such as pipeline inspections and equipment monitoring.</p></p>
