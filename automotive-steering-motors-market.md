<p><p>The automotive steering motors market primarily includes two types: Electric Power Steering (EPS) and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS). EPS uses electric motors to assist steering, offering precision and energy efficiency, while EHPS combines hydraulic systems with electric motors to enhance steering feel and responsiveness. Both systems aim to improve vehicle handling, reduce fuel consumption, and enhance driver comfort. The growing demand for advanced driver assistance systems and fuel-efficient technologies is driving the adoption of these steering solutions.</p></p>
