<p><p>The inhalation therapy nebulizer market comprises three main types: pneumatic, ultrasonic, and mesh nebulizers. Pneumatic nebulizers utilize compressed air to convert liquid medication into aerosol for inhalation, making them popular for home and clinical use. Ultrasonic nebulizers employ high-frequency sound waves to create mist, offering a quieter operation and faster delivery. Mesh nebulizers feature a vibrating mesh that helps produce a fine aerosol, optimizing medication delivery while being compact and portable. Each type serves varied patient needs and preferences in respiratory care.</p></p>
