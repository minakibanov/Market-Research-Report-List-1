<p><p>The PET plastic recycling market comprises two main types: chemical and mechanical recycling. Mechanical recycling involves the physical process of shredding and reprocessing PET materials into usable forms, maintaining their properties but often limited to a few cycles. In contrast, chemical recycling breaks down PET into its basic chemicals, allowing for virtually infinite recycling potential and the production of virgin-quality material. Both methods aim to reduce waste and improve sustainability in the plastic industry, catering to different applications and market needs.</p></p>
