<p><ul><li>SMD Type</li><li>Through Hole Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive thick film resistor market is divided into two main types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) and Through Hole types. SMD resistors are compact, allowing for high-density circuit designs and are commonly used in modern automotive electronics for their efficiency and reliability. In contrast, Through Hole resistors feature leads that pass through the circuit board, offering increased durability and ease of handling. Both types are essential for automotive applications, providing precise resistance values in demanding environments and contributing to overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
