<p><ul><li>Homopolymer Type</li><li>Copolymer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PP Compounds market is primarily divided into two types: Homopolymer and Copolymer. Homopolymer types are formed from a single type of polypropylene, offering excellent rigidity and strength, making them suitable for applications like automotive parts and containers. In contrast, Copolymer types consist of at least two different monomers, providing enhanced flexibility and impact resistance, ideal for products requiring durability and toughness. Each type caters to specific industries, ensuring diverse applications in packaging, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
