<p><p>Luminous surfaces are categorized into traditional and LED markets. Traditional luminous surfaces use fluorescent or incandescent technologies, providing soft illumination but often consuming more energy and having shorter lifespans. In contrast, the LED market leverages advanced light-emitting diode technology, offering enhanced energy efficiency, longer lifespans, and versatile designs. LED luminous surfaces are gaining popularity for their adaptability, cost-effectiveness, and lower environmental impact, making them ideal for both residential and commercial applications. This evolution reflects a shift towards sustainable lighting solutions.</p></p>
