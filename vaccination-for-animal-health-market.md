<p><p>The animal health vaccination market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Live Attenuated Vaccination** uses weakened pathogens to provoke a strong immune response without causing disease. </p><p>2. **Inactivated Vaccination** involves killed pathogens, providing immunity without the risk of infection. </p><p>3. **DNA Vaccination** introduces genetic material from pathogens to stimulate immune responses on a molecular level. </p><p>4. **Other Vaccination Types** encompass newer technologies like mRNA and subunit vaccines, expanding options for disease prevention in animals. Each type plays a key role in maintaining animal health and preventing disease outbreaks.</p></p>
