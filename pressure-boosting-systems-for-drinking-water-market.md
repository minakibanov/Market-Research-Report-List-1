<p><p>Pressure boosting systems for drinking water enhance water pressure in distribution networks. Single speed controlled pump systems operate at a constant speed and are energy-efficient for stable demands. Single fixed speed pump systems maintain a fixed speed, ensuring reliable pressure but may waste energy during low demand. Multi speed controlled pump systems adjust their speed based on demand, optimizing energy use. Multi fixed speed pump systems offer several fixed speed settings, providing flexibility but potentially leading to inefficiencies in varying demand conditions.</p></p>
