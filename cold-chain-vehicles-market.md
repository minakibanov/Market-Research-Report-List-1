<p><p>The cold chain vehicles market primarily includes refrigerated trailers and refrigerated trucks, both essential for transporting temperature-sensitive goods. Refrigerated trailers, often towed by trucks, are designed for bulk transport and feature advanced insulation and refrigeration systems, making them ideal for long-distance shipping. Refrigerated trucks, on the other hand, are standalone vehicles equipped with built-in refrigeration units, suitable for shorter routes or urban deliveries. Both types ensure that perishable items like food and pharmaceuticals maintain their quality and safety during transit.</p></p>
