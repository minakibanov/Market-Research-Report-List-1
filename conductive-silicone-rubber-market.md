<p><p>The conductive silicone rubber market is categorized into thermally conductive and electrically conductive types. Thermally conductive silicone rubber is designed to efficiently transfer heat, making it ideal for applications in electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors where temperature management is crucial. Electrically conductive silicone rubber, on the other hand, is used to dissipate static electricity and provide electromagnetic interference (EMI) shielding, essential in electronic device manufacturing and protective equipment. Both types offer versatility and advanced performance in various applications.</p></p>
