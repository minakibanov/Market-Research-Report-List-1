<p><p>Throttle Position Sensors (TPS) are critical components in automotive engines, monitoring the position of the throttle valve to optimize fuel injection and emissions control. The market is primarily segmented into two types: end switches and potentiometer types. End switch TPS provide a binary signal to indicate fully open or closed throttle positions, while potentiometer TPS offer a continuous variable output, enabling precise throttle position readings. Both types play essential roles in enhancing engine performance and fuel efficiency in modern vehicles.</p></p>
