<p><p>The Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometers market primarily includes two types: Flame Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometers (FAAS) and Electrothermal Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometers (ET AAS). FAAS utilizes a flame to vaporize and atomize samples, making it suitable for analyzing higher concentrations of metals. In contrast, ETAAS employs electrothermal techniques, allowing for more sensitive detection of trace elements with lower sample volumes. Both types are essential in environmental testing, food safety, pharmaceuticals, and metallurgy for precise elemental analysis.</p></p>
