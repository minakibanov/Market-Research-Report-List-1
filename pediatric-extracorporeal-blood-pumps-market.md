<p><p>The pediatric extracorporeal blood pumps market encompasses various types designed for neonatal and pediatric patients. Roller pumps utilize rollers to compress tubing, delivering precise flow rates. Centrifugal pumps generate flow through rotational motion, ensuring continuous flow with less trauma. Pulsatile pumps mimic natural heartbeats, offering a rhythmic flow beneficial for sensitive patients. Non-occlusive pumps minimize vessel occlusion, reducing potential complications. Other emerging technologies in the market include magnetic and diaphragm pumps, enhancing adaptability and efficiency in pediatric cardiac care.</p></p>
