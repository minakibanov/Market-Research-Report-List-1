<p><strong>The Infrared Night Vision Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Imaging</li><li>Near Infrared Imaging</li><li>Low Light Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared night vision camera market encompasses three main types: thermal imaging, near-infrared imaging, and low light imaging. Thermal imaging detects heat patterns, enabling visibility in total darkness and adverse weather, making it ideal for surveillance and search operations. Near-infrared imaging uses available light in the near-infrared spectrum for enhanced visibility in low-light conditions, suitable for security and military applications. Low light imaging amplifies existing light to produce clear images in dim environments, often used in security and wildlife observation.</p></p>
