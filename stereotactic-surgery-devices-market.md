<p><p>The stereotactic surgery devices market comprises three primary technologies: Gamma Knife, Proton Beam, and Linear Accelerator (LINAC) machines. Gamma Knife utilizes focused gamma radiation to precisely target brain lesions with minimal impact on surrounding tissues. Proton Beam therapy employs protons to deliver targeted radiation, offering advantages in treating deep tumors while sparing healthy tissue. LINAC machines generate high-energy X-rays or electrons, enabling versatile treatment options for various cancers, including precise tumor targeting and adjustable radiation doses.</p></p>
