<p><strong>The AR Optical Waveguide Engine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Geometric Waveguides</li><li>Diffractive Waveguides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AR Optical Waveguide Engine market encompasses two primary types: geometric waveguides and diffractive waveguides. Geometric waveguides utilize precise physical structures to guide light, often leveraging total internal reflection, making them suitable for applications requiring high image quality and clarity. In contrast, diffractive waveguides manipulate light through diffraction patterns, allowing for lighter and potentially more flexible designs. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing augmented reality experiences by effectively directing visual information to the user’s eyes.</p></p>
