<p><p>The OTP hardware authentication market is categorized primarily into three types: USB tokens, SIM tokens, and mini tokens. USB tokens are portable devices that connect to computers, providing secure two-factor authentication. SIM tokens are embedded in mobile devices, using the SIM card for authentication, making them convenient for smartphones. Mini tokens are compact devices that generate one-time passwords, easily carried on keychains for access to secure systems. Each type enhances security by offering reliable, physical means of authentication.</p></p>
