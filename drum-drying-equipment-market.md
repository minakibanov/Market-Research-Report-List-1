<p><strong>The Drum Drying Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Drum Drying Equipment</li><li>Double Drum Drying Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drum drying equipment market is primarily categorized into single drum and double drum drying equipment. Single drum dryers feature one large cylindrical drum, ideal for processing viscous materials, offering efficient heat transfer and easy operation. In contrast, double drum dryers consist of two parallel drums, allowing for higher production capacities and quicker drying times. Both types are widely used in food, chemical, and pharmaceutical industries, facilitating the conversion of slurries into dried products while maintaining quality and reducing moisture content.</p></p>
