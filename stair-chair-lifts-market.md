<p><p>Stair chair lifts are designed to assist individuals with mobility issues in navigating stairs safely and comfortably. The two main types are straight chair lifts and curved chair lifts. Straight chair lifts are ideal for staircases without curves or landings, providing a direct, linear path. In contrast, curved chair lifts are custom-designed to accommodate staircases with bends, turns, or multiple levels, ensuring seamless navigation. Both types enhance accessibility and independence for users in their homes.</p></p>
