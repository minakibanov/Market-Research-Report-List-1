The growing demand for advanced battery technologies, particularly in electric vehicles (EVs), consumer electronics, and renewable energy storage, underpins the expansion of the FEC market.</p><p>The Lithium Battery Electrolyte Fluoroethylene Carbonate (FEC) market is poised for significant growth, with an expected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 14.9% during the forecast period.
