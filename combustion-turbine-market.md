<p><p>The combustion turbine market is categorized into three main types: Heavy Duty, Light Duty, and Micro turbines. Heavy Duty turbines are designed for large-scale power generation with high efficiency and durability, ideal for continuous operation in utilities and industrial applications. Light Duty turbines are smaller, focusing on flexibility and quick-start capabilities, often used in peaking power plants. Micro turbines, the smallest in this sector, are suited for distributed generation and combined heat and power applications, offering efficiency in localized energy needs.</p></p>
