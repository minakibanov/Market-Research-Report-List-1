<p><p>The Flexible LED Panel Market primarily consists of two types: fiberboard-based and metal-based panels. Fiberboard-based panels are lightweight, cost-effective, and suitable for indoor applications, offering good insulation and easy installation. Conversely, metal-based panels are durability-focused, providing enhanced heat dissipation and structural integrity, making them ideal for outdoor or high-performance settings. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences, with fiberboard appealing to budget-conscious users and metal-based options catering to those requiring robust and long-lasting solutions.</p></p>
