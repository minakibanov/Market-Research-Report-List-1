<p><strong>The Automatic Speech Recognition Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Speaker-Dependent Speech Recognition System</li><li>Speaker-Independent Speech System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) market encompasses two primary types: speaker-dependent and speaker-independent systems. Speaker-dependent systems require users to train the model using their voice, enhancing accuracy for individual users, often used in personal devices. In contrast, speaker-independent systems are designed to recognize speech from any user without prior training, suitable for broader applications like virtual assistants and transcription services. The choice between these systems largely depends on the desired accuracy and the intended use case.</p></p>
