<p><p>The automatic wood chipper market is primarily divided into three types: drum-style, disc-style, and others. Drum-style chippers utilize a rotating drum equipped with sharp blades, delivering efficient processing of larger wood materials. Disc-style chippers feature a flat disc with knives, ideal for precise chipping and producing uniform chip sizes, often favored for smaller branches. The "other" category includes specialized chippers, such as portable or mobile units, catering to diverse applications and varying customer needs in the wood processing industry.</p></p>
