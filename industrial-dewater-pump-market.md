Submersible pumps are designed to operate while submerged in liquid, making them ideal for deep extraction applications. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move fluids and are widely used for their efficiency in transporting large volumes of water. Other types encompass diaphragm, screw, or peristaltic pumps, tailored for specific dewatering needs, offering versatility for different industrial applications in managing excess water effectively.</p></p>
