<p><p>The sanitary screw pump market features two primary types: vertical and horizontal screw pumps. Vertical screw pumps are designed for efficient operation in confined spaces, utilizing a vertical orientation that allows for gravity discharge and is ideal for high-viscosity fluids. In contrast, horizontal screw pumps operate on a horizontal axis, facilitating easier maintenance and installation while effectively handling a variety of fluids. Both types are essential in industries such as food processing and pharmaceuticals, where hygiene and efficient fluid transfer are critical.</p></p>
