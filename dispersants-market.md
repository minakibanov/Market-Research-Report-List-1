<p><p>Dispersants are chemical additives used to improve the stability of suspensions and emulsions. The market can be categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Anionic Type**: These dispersants carry a negative charge and are effective in stabilizing negatively charged particles, often used in water-based formulations. </p><p>2. **Cationic Type**: These carry a positive charge, suitable for stabilizing positively charged particles and used in applications like coatings and textiles.</p><p>3. **Others**: This category includes non-ionic and amphoteric dispersants, which offer versatility for various applications across industries such as paints, inks, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
