<p><p>The courier tape market primarily comprises single-sided adhesive tapes and double-sided adhesive tapes. Single-sided adhesive tapes feature adhesive on one side, making them ideal for sealing packages and securing items. In contrast, double-sided adhesive tapes have adhesive on both sides, facilitating bonding between surfaces without visible tape, commonly used in mounting applications.
