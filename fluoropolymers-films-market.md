<p><p>The fluoropolymer films market includes several types, each with unique properties. Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) offers excellent chemical resistance and high-temperature stability. Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene (FEP) is known for its clarity and ease of processing. Perfluoroalkoxy Alkane (PFA) combines the benefits of PTFE and FEP, providing flexibility. Polyvinyl Fluoride (PVF) is used for its durability and weather resistance, while Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF) features excellent mechanical strength. Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene (ETFE) is lightweight and highly resistant to UV and chemical exposure. Other fluoropolymer films cater to specialized applications.</p></p>
