<p><p>The High-frequency Copper Clad Laminate market is segmented into various types based on loss characteristics and application. UL-L (Ultra Low Loss) laminates are designed for high-performance applications requiring minimal signal attenuation. VL-L (Very Low Loss) offers slightly higher losses but remains suitable for critical RF applications. L-L (Low Loss) is used in standard RF circuits, while Mid-L (Mid Loss) caters to moderate performance needs. Std-L (Standard Loss) laminates are economical options for general-purpose applications, balancing cost and performance.</p></p>
