<p><p>The Plastic Surgery Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) market is segmented into three main types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, which utilize a single electrode to cut and coagulate tissue; Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, which have two electrodes that work together, providing precise control with minimal thermal spread; and Vessel Sealing Units, designed specifically for sealing blood vessels and tissues through electrically induced fusion. Each type serves distinct surgical needs, enhancing precision, safety, and efficacy in plastic surgery procedures.</p></p>
