<p><p>The melt pressure sensors market features two main types: NaK filled melt pressure sensors and oil filled melt pressure sensors. NaK filled sensors utilize a sodium-potassium alloy to provide enhanced sensitivity and durability, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. Conversely, oil filled sensors employ a hydraulic fluid, offering better stability and resistance to aggressive environments. Both types are crucial for monitoring processes in plastics and polymer production, ensuring optimal performance and product quality.</p></p>
