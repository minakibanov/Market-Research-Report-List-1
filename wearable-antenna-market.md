<p><p>The wearable antenna market is categorized into various frequency bands: L-band, S-band, C-band, and X-band. L-band operates between 1-2 GHz, suitable for GPS and mobile communications. S-band (2-4 GHz) is used in radar and satellite communications. C-band (4-8 GHz) supports satellite communications and Wi-Fi applications, while X-band (8-12 GHz) is utilized in military radar, satellite, and terrestrial communication systems. Each band has unique applications, influencing the design and functionality of wearable antennas for diverse user needs.</p></p>
