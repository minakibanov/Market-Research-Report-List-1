<p><p>The Iron Core Reactors market is categorized into two primary types: Dry Type and Oil Immersed Type. Dry Type reactors utilize air as a cooling medium, making them environmentally friendly and suitable for indoor applications. In contrast, Oil Immersed Type reactors use oil for cooling and insulation, offering enhanced thermal performance and efficiency in high-power applications. Each type serves distinct operational needs, with varying advantages in terms of maintenance, safety, and environmental impact.</p></p>
